About Us

At TTG, we cater to both novice and veteran Warhammer hobbyists. We stock a comprehensive range of Warhammer Age of Sigmar and Warhammer 40,000 miniatures, from core sets to the latest releases. Our extensive paint selection includes Citadel paints, Washes, and Technical paints to bring your creations to life. New to the hobby? Don't worry!
